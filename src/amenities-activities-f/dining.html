Dining at Amanpulo

Savor gourmet dishes with fresh, local ingredients at Amanpulo’s beachfront restaurants, private dining spots, and sunset bar, offering breathtaking ocean views.

Japanese

The Lagoon Club

A seemingly endless white-sand beach fringes the island’s secluded east coast, home to the Lagoon Club and its gracious pavilion. The Lagoon Club is open seasonally.

Mediterranean

Beach Club

Situated on the western side of Pamalican island directly on the sand, the Beach Club is the perfect spot for sunset cocktails, it specialises in Mediterranean cuisine and seafood. Open seasonally.

Italian

The Picnic Grove

A casual, open-air lunch venue, The Picnic Grove lies just metres from the beach under a canopy of trees. The Picnic Grove is open seasonally.

Filipino & International

Clubhouse Restaurant

Experience a culinary fusion, seamlessly blending Filipino specialities with steaks and club classics. The restaurant’s elegant ambiance of indoor and outdoor poolside dining is available throughout the day.

Barbecue

Destination Dining at Amanpulo

Privacy is a cornerstone of the Aman experience, and Amanpulo offers many pockets of seclusion in which to enjoy a personalised menu with a loved one or close friends.

Dining Experiences

Kawayan Bar

Enjoy a picnic on this floating bamboo bar, purpose built for all-day lounging between bouts of swimming and snorkelling. Incremental charges will apply.

Private Dining

Secluded locations for private picnics abound on the island, whether in a seaside cover or high up on the hillside with never-ending sea views.

Organic Farm

Discover the island's green heart, where dedicated gardeners grow fresh fruit, vegetables and herbs for our restaurant’s seasonal menus. Chef's visit daily, to handpick what they need in this verdant sanctuary.
