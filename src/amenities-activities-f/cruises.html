Cruising Experiences

Amanpulo’s large fleet of boats is available for a variety of activities and excursions, as well as private cruise charters. Cruise around the Palawan waters, or stop at offshore reefs for snorkelling; fish in the turquoise waters guided by the resort’s expert staff; or enjoy lunch as you drift along in the cooling breeze.

Manamoc Sandbar Picnic

A romantic adventure awaits guests at the Manamoc sandbar. Located on the edge of Manamoc Island, this sandbar is accessible only at certain tide conditions and is the perfect spot for a cozy beach lunch, for a natural and raw experience, with the comfort of Aman service. Private snorkeling following the lunch makes for an unforgettable experience. The excursion is available seasonally from December through to May and is dependent on weather conditions.

Explore Manamoc

Journey to nearby Manamoc Island, home to a good number of Amanpulo team members. Exploring the village of Manamoc allows guests to understand the simple way of life on these islands, to see the culture, and the symbiotic relationship between Manamoc and Amanpulo. By participating in the Manamoc Tour, a portion of the price is donated to the Andres Soriano Foundation to aid in developing livelihood within the community.

Cruising experiences at Amanpulo

The changing hues of Pamalican

Explore the changing hues of the waters surrounding Pamalican Island from the pontoon boat as the sun sets. Moonlight and starlight cruises are also available, offering a different perspective on the island; return to a private barbecue and bonfire at your Casita.

A varied fleet

Amanpulo’s fleet includes several motor boats as well as a pontoon boat and a number of sailboats. Whether it is island hopping, sailing the cooling winds or cruising in the pristine waters, Amanpulo’s watercraft are at the ready, with instruction and guides available to accompany you
