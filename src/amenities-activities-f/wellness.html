Wellness & Fitness at Amanpulo

Amanpulo is a place of peace, where relaxation and wellness go hand in hand.

Traditional healing arts of the Philippines

One of many soothing therapies available in the spa, the Hilot massage alternates hot and cold to rebalance areas of tension, making use of banana leaves and cold-pressed coconut oil.

A peaceful space for quiet contemplation

Enjoy a private session with one of our expert yoga or meditation guides in our tranquil yoga studio or in a quiet jungle glade close to the shore. Daily group classes are also offered.

Channeling hope

This full body massage is named Pag-asa, after the Filipino for ‘hope’. Using the cumulative healing knowledge of our expert therapists, it aims to channel intuition and kindness to heal and rejuvenate. Proceeds benefit the Andres Soriano Foundation, which supports education, healthcare and financial sustainability on Manamoc Island.
