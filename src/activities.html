Experiences at Amanpulo

Fill your days with aquatic adventures from kite surfing and scuba diving, to snorkelling and kayaking, or experience all the on-shore activities Amanpulo has to offer including tennis, cycling, bird watching and a jungle obstacle course. At night, get up close to the stars with our astronomical refracting telescope.

Things To Do at Amanpulo

Guests can enjoy a variety of activities, including snorkeling and diving in vibrant coral reefs, island hopping, paddleboarding, and kayaking.

Adventure seekers can go windsurfing, kitesurfing, or explore nature trails.
